    Motor Insurance Cover

    A motor insurance policy offers coverage to the insured vehicle against financial losses due to an accident or one resulting from other damages.
    This policy covers the policyholder in case of financial losses resulting from an accident or other damages sustained by the insured vehicle. A comprehensive motor insurance policy covers damages to third-party and third-party property along with compensating for own losses as well.
    According to Sec 146 of the Motor Vehicles Act, 1988, it is imperative that you purchase motor insurance prior to taking your vehicle out on the roads. Should you not want to buy a comprehensive insurance plan, getting third-party policy coverage is the bare minimum that you are mandated to acquire for your vehicle to ply.
